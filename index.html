Horror

My favorite Horror Movies are IT, The Conjuring universe, The Shining, The Exorcist. Sadly, no good horror movies have been made in the past 10 years. If you have recommendations, feel free to mail me on my socials. You can check them out at the bottom of the page.

Fantasy

My favorite Movies regarding the Fantasy genre are the Witcher series, Game of Thrones (except for the last season) and lord of the ring. I especially like the Witcher series with the games, books and the series.

Science-Fiction

I particularly like the film interstellar as it deals with the subject of black holes in relation to space and time. In theory, it is possible to travel in time if you can muster enough energy to bend space. The Alien Saga is pretty old but gold!

Drama

A beautiful mind deals with the mathematician John Forbes Nash, who was both brilliant and insane because he suffered from chizophrenia. I think you have to be a bit insane to be that awesome. I grew up watching braveheart and have a general weakness for historical dramas.
